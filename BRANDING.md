# KiplyStart Branding Guidelines

## Core Identity
**Name**: KiplyStart
**Mission**: Empower businesses and entrepreneurs with easy, customizable software.
**Vibe**: Modern, Fast, Tech-forward, Empowering.

## Color Palette

### Primary (Indigo)
Used for primary actions, gradients, and brand recognition.
- **Indigo 500**: `#6366f1` (Main)
- **Indigo 600**: `#4f46e5` (Hover/Active)

### Secondary (Emerald)
Used for success states, growth highlights, and "Start" accents.
- **Emerald 500**: `#10b981` (Accent)
- **Emerald 600**: `#059669` (Hover)

### Backgrounds (Dark Mode)
- **Slate 900**: `#0f172a` (Main Background)
- **Slate 800**: `#1e293b` (Cards/Sections)

## Typography
**Font Family**: `Inter` (Google Fonts)
- **Headings**: Bold / ExtraBold
- **Body**: Regular / Medium

## Logo Usage
The logo combines the letter 'K' with an abstract rocket/launch arrow, symbolizing the "Start" in KiplyStart.
- Use the gradient version on dark backgrounds.
- Ensure 20px padding around the logo.
